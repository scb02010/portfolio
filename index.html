Sarah Buchhorn


About Me

Hello! I'm interested in opportunities to build and utilize my skills in challenging, vibrant, and justice-centered environments. My background in physical science, youth mentoring, and urban planning gives me confidence in a variety of settings, and I love bringing multiple sources of knowledge together to better understand and tackle local issues.

Experience

Chicago Housing Authority (CHA)

Sustainability Manager

At CHA I develop and communicate sustainability goals, strategy, and metrics for the CHA's 12,500 unit public housing portfolio. I manage projects and initiatives including incentifized energy retrofits, utility budgeting, emissions reduction planning and reporting for CHA's Better Climate Challenge commitment, energy supply contracting, and landscaping and electrification guideline development.

Chicago Metropolitan Agency for Planning (CMAP)

Associate Analyst

At CMAP I collect, prepare, and analyze data using Python, GIS, and other tools to support agency travel and emissions modeling. I develop interactive and static visuals, create and update model input datasets and processes, and collaborate with others on climate change mitigation planning and water demand forecasting.

I also volunteer with our Future Leaders in Planning summer program, and served on the office social committee from 2019-2020. A few projects are on my GitHub.

U.S. Army Corps of Engineers - Construction Engineering Research Laboratory (CERL)

Research Assistant

As a Graduate Research Assistant, I worked with a small team to collect and process U.S. Army water meter records and characterize water usage to target conservation efforts. I also evaluated the feasibility of using harvested rainwater for irrigating sports fields at Fort Leonard Wood.

Champaign-Urbana Mass Transit District (CUMTD)

Safe Routes to School Intern

I worked closely with the leader of the Safe Soutes to School program to plan local events such as Walk 'n Roll to School Day and after-school workshops. I shared program updates and resources through our Weebly website and collected and prepared visuals for survey data on dangerous intersections.

Schuler Scholar Program

AmeriCorps Scholar Coach

During two years as a Scholar Coach at North Chicago Community High School, I met with students one-on-one and in small groups for math and science enrichment. I developed custom lessons, handouts, and presentations, and worked to establish a math-positive atmosphere on site. I helped students build self and social awareness through a seminar series titled "Exploring Justice" and educational and cultural trips in the area.

Transportation Emissions Analysis

At CMAP I worked to better understand, communicate, and improve our regional and local emissions modeling. While CMAP reported on PM2.5 and ozone for years, with the growing interest in greenhouse gas emissions we took the opportunity to revisit and refamiliarize stakeholders with our modeling process. To develop this presentation, I used a large dataset of emissions results from years of MOVES runs to uncover and explore trends and developed custom visuals to explain results. I delivered this presentation to several CMAP committees.
